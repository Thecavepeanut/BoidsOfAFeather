Boids of a feather!

Boids of a feather is a small d3 application that takes a look at swarm interactions. The idea is to let a boid of the same flock get closer than boids from other flocks, and to only follow/keep up with its flock. The app randomly places 8 flocks of 20 boids, thus scattering everything. The boids then try to meet up with only their flock, while giving extra room to anything that is not of the same feather. Eventually order will reign and all the flocks are nicely clumped until they collide. The collisions are probably my favorite part and remind me of electron density diagrams. P.S. If you don't like the color of the boids just refresh the page.
